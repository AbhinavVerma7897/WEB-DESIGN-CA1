Welcome to the DJI DRONE Launch Page

DJI manufactures commercial unmanned aerial vehicles (drones) for aerial photography and videography. It also designs and manufactures camera systems, gimbal stabilizers, propulsion systems, enterprise software, aerial agriculture equipment, and flight control systems. SZ DJI Technology Co., Ltd.

Key Features

Key features of DJI drones include high-quality cameras capable of capturing detailed photos and videos, advanced flight controllers with GPS for stable flight, a variety of intelligent flight modes for automated shots, obstacle avoidance sensors for enhanced safety, long-range video transmission systems, and foldable designs for portability. Different models also offer features like extended battery life, vertical shooting, and built-in storage to suit various user needs and levels of expertise.

CAMERA AND QUALITY

  • High-Resolution Cameras: Many DJI drones feature high-quality cameras, some with mechanical shutters and adjustable apertures for superior image quality.
  • HDR Video: Models like the DJI Mini 3 record detailed 4K HDR videos, enhancing dynamic range for richer footage.
  • Vertical Shooting: The DJI Mini 3 supports vertical shooting, ideal for social media content.
  • Stabilization: DJI drones often come with 3-axis gimbals that provide smooth and stable footage, even in windy conditions.
  • Intelligent Flight Modes: Features like ActiveTrack, QuickShots, and Panorama modes allow users to capture complex shots easily.
